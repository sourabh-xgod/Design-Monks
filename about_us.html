our philosophy

discovery

The begining of our design process always involves getting to know our clients. We listen to how you plan to use your spaces, how you look, how you entertain. We spend time on the site to understand how best to maximize views, natural light and breezes, whule creating a strong connection with the natural environment.

technology

From the earliest concepts through final design, Design Monks tightly integrate 3D modeling into the archetictural design process. Using Exterior and Interior 3D imaging and life exportable computer models.

design

We view our design process as a true collaboration between ourselves and our clients. All of those our designs are unique, a product of individuality of our clients, their families and friends. Our goal is to creat an environment that fits our clients objectives and lifestyle.
